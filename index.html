Meet Mamamoo

Mamamoo (마마무, Stylized as MAMAMOO) is a South Korean girl group formed in 2014 under Rainbow Bridge World (formerly known as WA Entertainment). The debuted on June 19, 2014 with the EP Hello and it is considered one of the best K-Pop debuts of 2014. The group are primarily known for their retro, jazz, R&B, and vocal performances.
